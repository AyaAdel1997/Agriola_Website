AGRIOLA is a platform that introduces remote sensing and deep learning to detect land cover change over the past 35 years. Monitoring agricultural land cover change in Delta Egypt using a computer vision-based deep learning model to detect and provide monthly insights of illegal actions mainly construction for decision-makers once satellite imagery is available. The next stage in our product will be land cover change forecasting. The web application provides change detection service along with a geospatial analytics platform to observe time series analysis over the years of water, vegetation, and NDVI. AGRIOLA also has a mobile application for precision agriculture services.

Why Us?

We provide change detection services with instant results using satellite images that are updated every two weeks, so you don’t have to wait for reports that are not frequently released.This service is not provided by our local competitors. We provide precision agriculture services such as detection of plant diseases simply by taking a photo using your phone, crop yield estimation so farmers can take precautions in case of an expected low crop. Our services are available in egypt with very affordable prices.
