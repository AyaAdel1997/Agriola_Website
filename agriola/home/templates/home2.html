OUR STORY

We're a group of young engineers, building the future of agriculture with technology. Supporting sustainable agriculture is our main concern so we decided to solve one of the most threatening problems facing our country and we choose land cover degradation and detection of plant diseases ,since they affect food availability and quality directly. That is why we created AGRIOLA.


Food affordability, quality and safety remain challenges as Egypt continues to rely on global markets for more than half of its staples. Malnutrition is another growing public health concern, with a 21.4 percent stunting rate, and 5.5 percent underweight rate of children under 5 years of age. The decreasing in the size of agricultural land could lead to catastrophic results if not stopped soon , as food availability is already a problem. One of the major factors leading to food insecurity is urban sprawl on agricultural lands.


Another problem is the ignorance of the size of the problem with no easy platform to aid educate farmers on it. So visual statistics and graphs are highly important for opening their eyes to the size of the problem, prompting them to take action and try to solve the problem or at least not make it worse. We also help provide a crop estimation so farmers could take precautions in case of an expected low crop of a certain yield ,such as what happened this year with the mango crop.



We employ satellite imagery aquired from Google Earth Engine.we used images from Landsat 7, Landsat 5 ,and sentinel to create our collections. Combined with Machine Learning ,they represent a great computational power.
