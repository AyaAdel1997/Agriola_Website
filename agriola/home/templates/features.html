Our FEATURES

This is what AGRIOLA provides to our customer

1. Urban vs. Vegetation change detection

This feature is concerned with detecting illegal construction on agricultural lands using satellite imagery and deep learning with an automated manner once the images are available every 2~4 weeks. The aim of this feature is to provide a fast detection method that enables governments to interfere as early as possible to terminate this construction. TRY NOW!

2. Plant disease detection

One of the biggest challenges for farmers is the prevention of disease appearance on crops. It is also important to reduce the spread of crop diseases as quickly as possible and in early stages of propagation, to enable farmers to attack them on time, or to remove the affected plants.

3. Weed and anomaly detection

We developed an image analysis portion of a robotic weed management system. Our system performs well enough to reliably detect the presence of weed vegetation and to correctly classify it by species with an accuracy of 92.3 % between eight classes of weed in images

4. Crop Counting

To get large and accurate data about wheat fields worldwide,image detection of "wheat heads"—spikes atop the plant containing grain is used. These images are used to estimate the density and size of wheat heads in different varieties. Farmers can use the data to assess health and maturity when making management decisions in their fields.

5. Analytics (Reports and crop yield estimation)
